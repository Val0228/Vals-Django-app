‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a module?

You can browse all available modules on our Modules page. Once you find a module you're interested in, simply click "Enroll in Module" on the module detail page. Make sure you're logged in to your student account.

How do I submit an assignment?

After enrolling in a module, you'll see a "Submit Assignment" button on the module detail page. Click it to upload your file and add any additional notes. You can update your submission at any time.

What file formats are accepted for submissions?

We accept PDF, DOC, DOCX, ZIP, RAR, and TXT file formats. Maximum file size is 10MB.

How are credits calculated?

Credits are awarded upon completion of modules. You need 50 credits to pass the year, 65 credits for a 2:1 classification, and all modules completed for a 1st class degree.

Can I cancel my enrollment?

Yes, you can cancel your enrollment at any time by visiting the module detail page and clicking "Cancel Enrollment". Please note that this action cannot be undone.
